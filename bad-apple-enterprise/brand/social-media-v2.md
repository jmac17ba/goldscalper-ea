## THE FIVE CONTENT PILLARS

### 1. THE BUILD
*Show the process. No filters on the grind.*

What it looks like:
- Screenshots of EA running on MT5 at 3am
- P&L updates (wins AND losses — the recovery is the story)
- Design mockups in progress
- Packaging arriving. Tags being sewn. Orders fulfilled.
- "nobody saw this coming" moments captured raw

Frequency: 3× per week
Formats: Reels, Stories, TikTok, X threads

---

### 2. YARD TO WORLD
*Jamaican roots. Global reach.*

What it looks like:
- Heritage design breakdowns (here's why we used this pattern)
- Patois moments — phrases, meaning, context
- Jamaica references in the design (madras cloth, headwrap, yard culture)
- Diaspora stories — tagging cities where the culture landed
- "Yard to World" as a recurring frame

Frequency: 2× per week
Formats: Carousel (Instagram), short-form video, community posts

---

### 3. DRESSED FOR THE WORK
*Product content. But make it honest.*

What it looks like:
- Chakka Collection worn by someone actually trading
- Quasheba Collection worn doing something real (gym, study, early morning)
- Corefuel in the gym, supplement on the desk next to the trading screen
- Not posed lifestyle shots — real context

Frequency: 2× per week
Formats: Reels, TikTok, product carousels

---

### 4. BUILT DIFFERENT
*The honey badger energy. Quiet, relentless, unbothered.*

What it looks like:
- Character content — Chakka and Quasheba animated/illustrated moments
- "honey badgers don't stop" narrative
- Comparison content (what they expected vs what we built)
- Member spotlights — who bought the bot, what they're building
- Contrarian takes on trading, fashion, hustle culture

Frequency: 1–2× per week
Formats: TikTok, Reels, X

---

### 5. PRECISION
*Education. The Academy content. Show the knowledge.*

What it looks like:
- EA strategy explainers (how the Pending-OCO works, what Fibonacci recovery means)
- Financial literacy snippets — short lessons from the Academy content
- "how to read a trade" breakdowns
- Market commentary tied back to the bot strategy
- Academy teasers before launch

Frequency: 2× per week
Formats: YouTube (long), TikTok/Reels (short), X threads

## MEMBERSHIP DROP STRATEGY

- Limited drops announced **members only first** (24hr head start)
- Public announcement happens after member window closes or if stock remains
- NFC tag reveal videos — "here's what happens when you scan it"
- Drop countdown on Stories (creates FOMO without overselling)
- Post-drop sold-out content builds scarcity reputation for next drop
