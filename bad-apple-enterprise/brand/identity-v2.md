## THE HERITAGE LAYER

Jamaica is not a trend in this brand — it is the root system.

**What goes in:**
- Bandana and madras cloth patterns (not tourist versions — actual traditional patterns)
- Headwrap silhouettes on Quasheba pieces (a crown, not a costume)
- Patois text in typography — real phrases, real spelling, not phonetic mockery
- "Yard to World" as the philosophical arc (from the island to the diaspora to everywhere they landed)

**What stays out:**
- Stereotyped colour combos (red/gold/green used lazily)
- Tourist-facing imagery
- Anything that performs Jamaica for outsiders rather than resonating with insiders

The diaspora will recognize it. The outsider will be drawn to it. The insider will respect it.

---

## THE QUANT LAYER

Every Chakka piece wears the market:
- Candlestick patterns as graphic prints (not decorative — actual OHLC candle shapes)
- Grid ladder (the Fibonacci recovery visual — rungs getting wider as you go deeper)
- Support/resistance lines as horizontal graphic elements
- Chart line on cap brims

This is for people who understand what they're looking at.
And for those who don't — it looks clean anyway.

## TYPOGRAPHY

**Hierarchy:**
1. **Display:** Bold sans-serif — clean, undecorated, says it loud
2. **Body:** Medium weight sans-serif — readable, no nonsense
3. **Accent:** Patois script or stylized hand lettering — heritage moments only, not overused

**Patois typography rules:**
- Use real patois (e.g. "Yard to World", "Nuh stop", "The work nah wait")
- Never phonetically mock the accent — spell it as Jamaicans spell it
- Use sparingly — the impact comes from restraint

## NFC/QR AUTHENTICATION SYSTEM

Every limited drop garment contains:
- **NFC chip** stitched into the inner label or hem
- **QR code** printed on the tag (backup to NFC)

On scan:
- Verifies item is authentic (chip registered to production run)
- Pings member account (logs the scan, confirms tier)
- Unlocks exclusive content (behind-the-scenes, next drop preview, EA update notes)

Counterfeit items have no chip → scan fails → immediately identifiable.
Each scan is logged → provenance trail → collector value.
