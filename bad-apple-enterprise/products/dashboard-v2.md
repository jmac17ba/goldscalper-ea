### POD APPLE — The Launch Vehicle

**Pod Apple** is the print-on-demand product line powering Phase 1 of all physical collections.

Every Chakka, Quasheba, and Corefuel item launches through Pod Apple first:
- Zero inventory risk
- Real demand signal before committing capital
- When a SKU hits 50+ units sold → graduates to in-house production
- In-house production = better margins + NFC/QR authentication tags stitched in

*Pod Apple = the seed. In-house = the tree.*

## AUTHENTICATION LAYER — NFC/QR System

- Every limited Phase 3 drop includes an NFC chip + QR code stitched into the garment
- Scan = pings member dashboard, verifies authenticity, unlocks exclusive content
- Counterfeits have no chip → immediately identifiable
- Collector value: each scan logs to member account (limited scan count = provenance trail)
