**17BA Bad Apple Enterprise | Founder: Jahnoi McIntosh | Jamaica**
**Updated: June 2026**

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Bad Apple Enterprise is a Jamaican-born global ecosystem brand built around two Expert Advisor
trading bots and a clothing/lifestyle brand funded by their revenue.

The ecosystem has four branches:
1. **The Bots** — Automated trading EAs for MT5 (XAUUSD primary)
2. **The Collections** — Chakka (men's) + Quasheba (women's) streetwear
3. **Corefuel** — Gym wear + white-label supplements via Suppliful
4. **The Academy** — Financial literacy education products

**The flywheel:** Trading income funds the brand. The brand builds the community.
The community buys the bots. The bots fund more brand. The loop compounds.

## MARKET POSITIONING

**Target customer:**
- Primary: 18–35, Jamaican diaspora (JA, UK, CA, US, JA)
- Secondary: Global grinders — anyone who trades, hustles, builds
- They dress with intent. They invest. They move in silence.

**Not competing with:**
- Fast fashion (H&M, ASOS)
- Luxury fashion (Gucci, Off-White)
- Generic activewear (Alo, Lululemon — original 2023 research preserved)

**Competing with:**
- Niche streetwear brands with story (Fear of God, Corteiz, NOCTA early era)
- Trading education platforms (but we also sell clothing)
- No one is doing this exact combination

**Competitive advantage:**
The bots fund the brand. No external investment needed.
The brand builds community that buys the bots.
The heritage layer (Jamaica) is authentic — it's not adopted, it's origin.
The membership mechanic creates exclusivity without artificial scarcity.
