No profit is guaranteed. We never promise returns. Bad Apple bots are tools for education and disciplined participation — not get-rich-quick schemes. Trading carries a real risk of losing money, including the possibility of losing more than you expected in fast-moving markets.

Trading is high-risk

Trading gold (XAUUSD) and foreign exchange is high-risk and speculative. Prices move fast and unpredictably. You should never trade with money you cannot afford to lose, and you should understand that past performance — including any backtest — does not predict future results.

How our bots can lose

Chakka and Quasheba use a breakout entry followed by a grid / Fibonacci recovery basket. This method adds positions on adverse moves to try to recover at a combined take-profit. In a strong, sustained trend that never retraces, a recovery basket can:

  • draw down heavily on your account balance,
  • reach the drawdown guard and close at a realized loss, or
  • — if you disable the guard — continue all the way to a margin call.

The drawdown guard reduces but does not eliminate this risk. No automated strategy can remove the risk of loss.

You are in control — and responsible

  • The bot runs on your own MetaTrader 5 / broker account. You keep custody of your funds; the bot only executes orders.
  • You choose the settings (levels, lot sizes, drawdown guard %). Your configuration is your responsibility.
  • Always test on a demo account and in the Strategy Tester before risking real funds.
